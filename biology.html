Procedure

  1. Set your light intensity to the desired value BEFORE turning on the lamp.
  2. Click "Turn on Lamp" to initiate photosynthesis.
  3. Set up a 15 second timer on your phone or using this link.
  4. Count the number of bubbles that appear in 15 seconds, and record that value.
  5. Repeat this step 2 more times for each light intensity level.
