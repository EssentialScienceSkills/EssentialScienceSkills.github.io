Fire Temperature

Procedure

  1. Select your desired substance.
  2. Do a "dry run". Click "Start Simulation", then set the fire temperature to the max. Note down the APPROXIMATE temperature where the substance changes from solid to liquid.
  3. For each of your three other trials, you can adjust the speed of temperature increase by changing the fire size on your bunsen burner. Try and find the exact temperature where this change occurs.
