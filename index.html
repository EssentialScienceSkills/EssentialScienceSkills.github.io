Why science?

By understanding science, you begin to unlock the secrets of the universe! Science is everywhere in the world around us. From your toothbrush, to your phone, to the food you eat. In addition to being successful in school, you can benefit from learning science by being able to wrap your head around every day life in a more systematic way.
